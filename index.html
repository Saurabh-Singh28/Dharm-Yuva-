About Sanatani IT Cell

The Sanatani IT Cell is a digital initiative of Dharm Yuva Sangathan (DYS), dedicated to supporting the Dharma ecosystem with digital solutions, tech support, and outreach. Our volunteers contribute to websites, content management, social media strategies, cybersecurity, and digital literacy for the Sanatan Dharma community.

What We Do

  • Technical support for Dharma projects
  • Managing websites, social platforms, and apps
  • Workshops: digital skills, cybersecurity, coding
  • Helpdesk and troubleshooting for DYS members
  • Building new tools for community outreach
  • Volunteer network of web developers and IT professionals
